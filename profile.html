About

Hi! I’m Tania, a makeup enthusiast passionate about exploring the art of beauty. My work ranges from creating natural, effortless looks that highlight everyday elegance to bold and imaginative fantasy makeup that tells a story. For me, makeup is more than a tool—it’s a medium for self-expression and creativity.

This portfolio is a showcase of my journey, blending techniques and ideas to bring visions to life. Take a moment to explore my creations, where simplicity meets imagination, and beauty knows no bounds.
